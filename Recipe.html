Tres Leches Cake

Ingredients
  • Yellow Cake Mix
  • 1‚ÅÑ2 Cup of Vegetable Oil
  • Cool Whip
  • La Lechera Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • Carnation Evaporated Milk
  • 1 Cup of 2% Milk
  • 3 Eggs

    • Step 1
    Follow the cake mix box insructions and pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees.

    Pour the batter into a pan and place it in the oven and bake for 20-30 minutes.

    Step 2
    Take the cake out from the oven, and let it sit until cool.

    Once cooled, cut off the entire crust and place the remaining cake in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
    Step 3
    In a blender, add 1 can of condensed milk and 1 can of evaporated milk, then fill the condensed can with normal 2% milk, and pour in the blender and blend.
    Poke holes to the top of the cake and pour the milk contents over the entire cake.
    Step 4
    Spread cool whip evenly on the entire cake.

    Place in refrigerator for up to 1-2 hours.
    Step 5


    Take the cake out and enjoy! Feel free to decorate it with any fruit of your liking.
